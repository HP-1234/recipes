Burger

Cooking Information

  • Prep Time = 5 Minutes
  • Cook Time = 15 Minutes
  • Total Time = 20 Minutes
  • Servings = 4

Description

This quick and easy recipe will feed the whole family, with 4 burger patties that you can whip up in no time flat. Share the delicious juicy, meaty, and precious burgers.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Burger buns
  • One whole onion
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Lettuce
  • Slices of American cheese
  • One egg
  • Salt and pepper
  • Bread crumbs
  • Mayonnaise

Cooking Steps

  1. Whisk the egg in a large bowl with salt and pepper
  2. Add ground beef and bread crumbs to bowl
  3. Form the mixture into 3/4 inch patties
  4. Place patties on grill
  5. Cook for 6 to 8 minutes each side
  6. Make sure beef is at least 160 degrees F
  7. Assemble burger
