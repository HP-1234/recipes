Boiled Egg

Cooking Information

  • Prep Time = 5 Minutes
  • Cook Time = 20 Minutes
  • Additional Time = 15 Minutes
  • Total Time = 40 Minutes
  • Servings = 8

Description

Have you always tried to get the perfect hard boiled egg, but you keep failing and cant seem to get it right. Well worry not, This guide will show you how to make the perfect boiled eggs!

Ingredients

  • 8 eggs
  • Pot of water
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Cooking Steps

  1. Add a small amount of vinegar to the water
  2. Bring your pot of water to a gentle boil
  3. Insert the eggs
  4. Let boil for 10-12 minutes
  5. Remove eggs and place in ice water
  6. Let cool for 15 minutes
  7. Once cooled, peel the eggs
  8. Cut eggs in half
  9. Add salt and pepper to taste
