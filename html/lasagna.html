Lasagna

Cooking Information

  • Prep Time = 30 Minutes
  • Cook Time = 2 Hours 30 Minutes
  • Additional Time = 15 Minutes
  • Total Time = 3 Hours 15 Minutes
  • Servings = 12

Description

This world famous lasagna recipe is over 2 decades old and was pioneered by the legendary John Chandler. This recipe garners over 7 million views per year and has blown the internet away with a new sensation of cheese, tomato sauce, and meat.

Ingredients

  • Sweet Italian sausage
  • Lean ground beef
  • One whole onion
  • Two cloves of garlic
  • Can of crushed tomatoes
  • Two cans of tomato sauce
  • Two cans of tomato paste
  • Two tablespoons of white sugar
  • Parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, black pepper, and fennel seeds
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese
  • One egg

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
